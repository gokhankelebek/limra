Our Story

Born from an ancient city by the sea, brought to your neighborhood.

Limra takes its name from a quiet corner of the Mediterranean coast — an old city of stone, sun, and shared meals. We've carried that spirit across the water and into Holly Springs, but we've translated it for the way you actually eat now: bright, fast, generous, and a little bit refined.

Help yourself to the buffet — fresh Mediterranean dishes, hot side and cold side, refreshed throughout the day. Watch the döner slice off the spit. Order a gyro kebab from the grill. Stop at the pastry case for baklava and warm börek. Finish (or start) at the espresso counter with a properly pulled cappuccino or macchiato.

Limra is a family business — opened by two chefs, Mehmetcan and Elif. Every dish that lands on the buffet, every tray of baklava, every espresso pulled at the counter passes through their hands first.

— Mehmetcan & Elif
